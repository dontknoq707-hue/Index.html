Frequently Asked Questions

What is MissionJeet?

MissionJeet is a mission to bring honesty and quality back into the JEE and NEET preparation ecosystem. We focus on delivering high-quality academic content through highly qualified and domain-expert educators, without any fake promises or rank purchasing schemes.

How is MissionJeet different from other coaching institutes?

Unlike other institutes, MissionJeet operates on principles of honesty and transparency. We have no rank purchasing system, no fake testimonials, and no star batch discrimination. Every student receives equal attention and quality education. Our motto is "Kisi Bacche Ka Nuksaan Nahi Hone Dena" - ensuring no student suffers due to false promises or poor quality content.

What are DRONA JEE and DRONA NEET programs?

DRONA JEE and DRONA NEET are our flagship yearlong batch programs designed for serious aspirants. These comprehensive programs include live classes, doubt-solving sessions, complete study material, mock tests, strategy sessions, and genuine mentorship from experienced educators and successful students.

Who are the educators at MissionJeet?

Our content is delivered by highly qualified and domain-expert educators who have proven track records in JEE and NEET coaching. They focus on concept clarity, strategic preparation, and ensuring students develop deep understanding rather than just memorization.

What study materials do you provide?

Students receive curated study material designed specifically for JEE and NEET preparation, including comprehensive notes, practice problems, previous year questions, mock tests, and strategy guides. All materials are developed by our expert educators to ensure quality and relevance.

How can I join MissionJeet's Telegram community?

You can join our Telegram channel by clicking the "Join Telegram" button available throughout our website. Our Telegram community provides daily updates, study tips, motivational content, important announcements, and a platform to connect with fellow aspirants and educators.

What is the fee structure for DRONA programs?

For detailed information about fee structure and payment options, please contact us through the form below or reach out to us through our Telegram channel. We believe in transparent pricing and offer various payment plans to make quality education accessible.

Do you provide doubt-solving support?

Yes! We provide comprehensive doubt-solving support through interactive sessions, dedicated doubt-clearing periods, and online platforms. Our educators ensure that no student is left with unclear concepts or unanswered questions.
